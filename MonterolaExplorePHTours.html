TOP 3 must-visit places in the Philippines

Luzon - Banaue Rice Terraces

They offer breathtaking views and a glimpse into indigenous culture and sustainable farming techniques.

Visayas - Chocolate Hills

It’s a top tourist attraction with scenic viewpoints and nearby adventure activities like ATV rides.

Mindanao - Siargao Island (Surigao del Norte)

The island also boasts lagoons, rock pools, and vibrant marine life, making it a paradise for adventure seekers.
